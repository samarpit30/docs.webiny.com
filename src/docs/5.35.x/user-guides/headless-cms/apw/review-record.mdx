In this tutorial, we will learn how you can review a record in a Publishing Workflow.

## Prerequisites

To follow this tutorial:

- You need to have the record **Elevate Your Style: 5 Stylish Ways to Wear a Black T-Shirt for Men** submitted for review. If you don't have it, please follow the [Submit Record for Review](./submit-record-for-review) tutorial to submit the record for review.

- You need to be logged in to your Webiny instance with a reviewer's account (we set reviewer(s) right in the [Define Workflow](./define-workflow) tutorial)

1. From the **Side Menu**, click **Publishing Workflows** > **Content Reviews**.

    ✔️ The **Content review dashboard** screen opens.

2. Click the **Elevate Your Style: 5 Stylish Ways to Wear a Black T-Shirt for Men** list item.

    ✔️ The record review screen for the record opens.

3. In front of the title **Elevate Your Style: 5 Stylish Ways to Wear a Black T-Shirt for Men**, click the **external link** icon.

    ✔️ The **Blog** editor screen opens in a new tab.

    **Note**: If you want to make any changes in the record by yourself, you can make them and save them by clicking **ENTRY PREVIEW**.

4. On the record review screen, click **➕ REQUEST CHANGE**.

    ✔️ The **Change request** screen opens.

5. In the **Title** textbox, type **Change blog's title for better SEO**.

6. In the **Body** textbox, type **Change the title of the blog to "Black T-Shirt Styling: 5 Timeless Looks for Men"**.

7. Click **SUBMIT**.

    ✔️ The message "Change request created successfully!" displays.
